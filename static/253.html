听力文本：

Scientists have recently revealed an instinct in women after they did a series of experiments and observations. They wanted to determine on which side women hold their babies and why.

First, it is found that modern mothers tend to hold their babies on the left. Of 255 right-handed mothers, 83 percent held the baby on the left. And out of 32 left-handed women, 78 percent held the baby on the left. However, when they watched women coming out of supermarkets with baby-sized packages, the packages were held with no preference.

Then, the researchers watched dental patients. They were given a large rubber ball to hold during treatment. The majority held the ball to their left side, even when it interfered with the dentist’s activities. This suggested that in times of stress objects are held against the left side.

Besides, 115 mothers were observed for how they held the babies after they had been separated from their babies for 24 hours after birth. The baby was directly presented to the mid-line of the mother’s body. 53 percent of the mothers placed the baby on the left and 47 percent on the right.

Scientists think left-handed holding enables the baby to hear the heartbeat. In order to discover whether hearing the heart has a beneficial effect on the baby, the sound of a human heartbeat was played to 102 babies in a New York nursery for four days. There was another group of babies who were not exposed to heartbeats. The babies listening to heartbeats gained much more weight and cried far less than the babies in the other group.
